Questions and answers

To register, use the "Registration" functionality on the site https://www.fabrikant.ru/ or contact the site managers using the contacts listed on the site.

The showcase displays objects for which the starting prices of property owners are indicated

To participate in the auction, you can:

  • contact the managers on the contacts indicated on the website or on the page of the object of sale
  • apply for participation using tips, links and forms on the site

On the main page of the showcase (catalog) there is a sorting functionality. You can sort the objects by the expiration of the acceptance of applications, by the date of publication, by the price of the lot.

How can I subscribe to the procedure change notifications? Notifications about changes in the procedure must be sent to all participants who have submitted applications for participation in the procedure.

A non-participant of the procedure can subscribe to the distribution of messages (including on changes) on a specific procedure in the following ways:

  • by adding the procedure to your favorites in the "Messaging Management" widget of your Personal Account;
  • by subscribing to the newsletter on the procedure directly on the notification page.

A filter is provided to search for objects in the showcase. You can search by category, by keywords, by price, by region. Also, in each category of the catalog there are additional filtering options in accordance with the characteristics of the category.

The list of objects admitted to auction is regulated by the norms of the legislation of the Russian Federation. The showcase has a wide range of property categories: various types of real estate, vehicles, equipment, goods, etc. If you want to sell an object, but do not find a suitable category, contact the site managers using the contacts listed on the site.

Subject to sale:

  • own property
  • non-core and inefficient assets
  • seized property
  • pledged property
  • bankrupt property
  • state and municipal property

Requesting information from managers

The card of each object for sale contains basic information, as well as the contacts of the manager, from whom you can clarify details and additional information about the property by phone or e-mail.

Application for participation in the auction or for the contact of the manager with you

Next to the description of the property there is an opportunity to submit an application. After its registration, managers will contact you on their own
