About the showcase

The showcase contains property for which auctions are organized at the Fabrikant ETP. Information about objects from reliable sellers cooperating with the site is allowed to be placed on the showcase. All information about the objects on the showcase is posted and moderated by site managers.

Showcase Benefits
  • quick search for information on the property of interest
  • convenient viewing of media materials and the location of objects
  • easy filtering by categories and parameters
  • list of upcoming auctions "at a glance"
  • visually convenient tracking of deadlines for bidding
Who is the showcase for?

The showcase will be useful to any market participants looking for profitable objects for purchase, investment, rent, as well as those who want to profitably sell or rent property.
